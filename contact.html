Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery times?

Standard delivery is 5-7 business days within the Benelux region. Express delivery options are available for urgent orders. International shipping times vary by destination.

Do you provide technical support?

Yes, our technical team is available Monday-Friday, 8:00-18:00 CET. We offer phone support, email assistance, and on-site consultations for complex installations.

Can I request product samples?

Yes, we provide samples for qualified business inquiries. Contact our sales team with your project requirements and we'll arrange appropriate samples.

What certifications do your products have?

All our products comply with European standards and carry relevant certifications including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, CE marking, and KIWA approvals.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes, we specialize in custom pipeline solutions. Our engineering team can design and manufacture products to meet specific project requirements.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment terms for established businesses including 30-day net terms, letters of credit, and payment plans for large projects.
