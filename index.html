About Us

Hydro Benelux is an export-oriented Benelux company for production of PE (polyethylene) and PP (polypropylene) pipes.

Situated in Brussels, Belgium, Hydro Benelux was founded in 1990 as a plant for production of tools and elements of injection molded plastic. Since then, it has grown to become one of the leading manufacturers in the region.

Following successful start aided by experience gained by successfully realized projects across Belgium, Netherlands and Luxembourg, today our company is export-oriented, and 95% of its products are exported to international markets across Europe and beyond.
