Product Description

HYDRO KAN DUCT - Cable Protection Systems

  • HDPE/PP corrugated and smooth wall cable ducts
  • Diameter range: DN 40mm to DN 200mm
  • Single-wall and double-wall construction
  • Colors: Red, orange, black (customizable)
  • Standards: EN 61386, IEC 60794-4
  • Lengths: 6m standard, coils available

General Properties

  • High crush resistance
  • UV stabilized for direct burial
  • Excellent cable pulling properties
  • Chemical resistant
  • Flexible and lightweight
  • Temperature range: -20°C to +60°C
  • Long service life (50+ years)
  • Easy installation and jointing

Applications

Fiber Optic Networks

Telecommunication and data cables

Electrical Cables

Power distribution and lighting

Traffic Systems

Traffic lights and control cables

Building Infrastructure

In-building cable management
