New Production Line Launched: Increasing Capacity by 30%

Hydro Benelux is proud to announce the successful commissioning of our state-of-the-art energy-efficient production line, marking a significant milestone in our company's growth and commitment to sustainable manufacturing.

Investment in the Future

After 18 months of planning and construction, our new production facility in Brussels has become operational. This €15 million investment represents the largest expansion project in Hydro Benelux's history and demonstrates our confidence in the growing European demand for high-quality PE and PP piping systems.

The new line is equipped with cutting-edge extrusion technology from leading European manufacturers, capable of producing pipes ranging from DN 110mm to DN 630mm in diameter. This expansion significantly increases our production capacity while reducing energy consumption by 25% compared to our previous generation equipment.

Advanced Technology

The production line incorporates several innovative features that set new standards in the industry:

  • Automated Quality Control: Inline ultrasonic wall thickness measurement and automated dimensional control ensure consistent product quality
  • Energy Recovery Systems: Heat recovery units capture and reuse thermal energy, reducing our carbon footprint
  • Smart Manufacturing: Industry 4.0 integration with real-time monitoring and predictive maintenance capabilities
  • Material Efficiency: Advanced scrap recycling systems minimize waste and support our circular economy goals

"This investment demonstrates our commitment to providing our customers with the highest quality products while minimizing environmental impact. The new line will enable us to better serve the growing infrastructure needs across Benelux and beyond."

— Marc Vermeulen, General Director, Hydro Benelux

Environmental Benefits

Sustainability was a key consideration in designing the new production line. In addition to the 25% reduction in energy consumption, the facility features:

  • Solar panels on the roof providing 20% of the facility's electricity needs
  • Rainwater collection systems for cooling water requirements
  • LED lighting with motion sensors throughout the production hall
  • Zero-waste-to-landfill through comprehensive recycling programs

These measures align with our commitment to achieving carbon-neutral operations by 2030, as outlined in our sustainability roadmap published earlier this year.

Job Creation and Training

The expansion has created 35 new positions across production, quality control, and technical support functions. All new team members have undergone comprehensive training programs, including certifications in advanced extrusion technology and quality management systems.

We have partnered with local technical schools and universities to develop apprenticeship programs, ensuring a pipeline of skilled workers for the future of manufacturing in Belgium.

Impact on Customers

Our customers will benefit from:

  • Reduced lead times with 30% increased production capacity
  • Enhanced product consistency through automated quality systems
  • Expanded product range with new diameter options
  • Improved delivery reliability with increased inventory capacity

Looking Ahead

This production line is the first phase of our modernization program. Phase 2, scheduled for 2025, will focus on expanding our fittings and accessories production capabilities. We are also exploring opportunities for automation and robotics integration to further improve efficiency and workplace safety.

The successful launch of this facility reinforces Hydro Benelux's position as a leading pipeline manufacturer in the Benelux region and strengthens our ability to compete in European and international markets.
