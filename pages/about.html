ABOUT HYDRO BENELUX

PIONEERING PIPE SOLUTIONS FOR URBAN DEVELOPMENT

Our Story

Hydro Benelux is an export-oriented Benelux company for production of PE (polyethylene) and PP (polypropylene) pipes.

Situated in Brussels, Belgium, Hydro Benelux was founded in 1990 as a plant for production of tools and elements of injection molded plastic. Since then, it has grown to become one of the leading manufacturers in the region.

Following successful start aided by experience gained by successfully realized projects across Belgium, Netherlands and Luxembourg, today our company is export-oriented, and 95% of its products are exported to international markets across Europe and beyond.

Mission

Promoting new technologies while adhering to stringent EU standards. With certified quality and competitive pricing, we embrace regulations and ecological responsibility.

Vision

A modern factory, a leader in the Benelux region for development and production of plastic products for infrastructure buildings, within the frames of an environmentally safe system with maximum safety for all stakeholders.

Values

At Hydro Benelux, we're dedicated to leading the industry with top-quality, eco-friendly pipes while prioritizing environmental protection and employee safety.

Our Journey

1990 - Beginnings

Hydro Benelux started manufacturing with one PE pipe production line and 10 employees in Brussels, Belgium.

1995 - Expansion

Added PP pipe production capabilities and doubled our workforce to meet growing European demand.

2000 - European Markets

Began exports to EU countries, establishing partnerships across Western and Central Europe.

2005 - Certification

Achieved ISO 9001, BENOR, and KIWA certifications, solidifying our commitment to quality.

2010 - Innovation

Introduced PE-100 RC advanced piping systems with superior crack resistance properties.

2015 - Growth

Reached 200+ employees and €50M annual turnover, becoming a market leader in Benelux.

2020 - Sustainability

Launched carbon-neutral production initiative and installed solar panels on factory roofs.

2025 - Leadership

Solidified position as the premier pipeline manufacturer in Benelux with 3,150+ completed projects.

Leadership

At Hydro Benelux, our mission has always been clear: to lead with innovation, deliver quality by European standards, and stay ahead of the curve in our industry. We are committed to creating sustainable solutions, expanding into new markets, and sharing knowledge with all who seek to grow. Together, we build not just for today, but for a future our next generations will be proud of.
