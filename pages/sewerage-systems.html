SEWERAGE SYSTEMS

ADVANCED SOLUTIONS FOR WASTEWATER MANAGEMENT

Product Description

HYDRO KAN Sewerage Systems

  • PP (Polypropylene) corrugated and solid wall pipes
  • Diameter range: DN 110mm to DN 1200mm
  • Stiffness classes: SN2, SN4, SN8, SN16
  • Lengths: 6m standard, custom lengths available
  • Standards: EN 13476, EN 1852, ISO 21138
  • Color: Orange-brown for visibility

General Properties

  • Excellent chemical resistance to sewage
  • High impact strength
  • Abrasion resistant
  • Temperature range: -40°C to +95°C
  • Lightweight and easy to install
  • Tight sealing with rubber gaskets
  • Long service life (100+ years)
  • Environmentally friendly and recyclable

Applications

Municipal Sewerage

Urban wastewater collection networks

Industrial Drainage

Chemical and industrial effluent systems

Storm Water

Rainwater drainage and management

Building Drainage

Internal and external building systems

HYDRO KAN Product Lines

HYDRO KAN - Corrugated PP Pipes

Double-wall corrugated polypropylene pipes for gravity sewerage systems. Outer corrugated wall provides structural strength, inner smooth wall ensures excellent flow characteristics.

Technical Specifications

Diameter (mm) Stiffness Class Length (m) Application
DN 110 SN8 6m Building drainage
DN 315 SN8 6m Municipal sewerage
DN 630 SN8 6m Main collectors
DN 1200 SN4 6m Large diameter sewers

HYDRO KAN SPIRAL - Spiral Wound Pipes

Large diameter spiral wound PP pipes for heavy-duty applications. Manufactured on-site or in factory with customizable diameters up to DN 3000mm.

Key Features

  • Diameters from DN 300mm to DN 3000mm
  • Custom lengths up to 12m or longer
  • Excellent ring stiffness (SN4 to SN16)
  • Ideal for deep installations
  • Cost-effective for large projects

HYDRO KAN PPHM - High Performance Pipes

Polypropylene High Modulus pipes with superior mechanical properties for demanding applications. Enhanced resistance to deformation and external loads.

Advanced Features

  • Higher stiffness with thinner walls
  • Better performance under heavy traffic loads
  • Reduced material usage (eco-friendly)
  • Suitable for trenchless installation
  • Temperature resistance up to 95°C continuous

DRAINAGE PIPES - Perforated Systems

Perforated PP pipes for land drainage, sports fields, and agricultural applications. Available with geotextile sock for filtration.

Specifications

  • Diameters: DN 65mm to DN 250mm
  • Perforation patterns: 360° or sectoral
  • With or without geotextile filter
  • Coil or straight lengths available
  • Ideal for subsurface drainage
