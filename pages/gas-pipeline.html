GAS PIPELINE SYSTEMS

SAFE & RELIABLE GAS DISTRIBUTION

Product Description

PE Pipes for Gas Distribution

  • Yellow/orange PE pipes for natural gas networks
  • Diameter range: DN 20mm to DN 630mm
  • Pressure ratings: PN 4 to PN 16 bar (MOP)
  • Materials: PE-80, PE-100, PE-100 RC
  • Standards: EN 1555, ISO 4437, DVGW
  • Pipe lengths: 12m bars, coils up to DN 63mm

Safety & Performance Features

  • Leak-proof welded joints
  • Corrosion resistant (no cathodic protection needed)
  • Flexible, earthquake resistant
  • No sparking or static electricity
  • UV stabilized for outdoor storage
  • Temperature range: -20°C to +40°C
  • Service life: 50+ years guaranteed
  • Fully traceable with marking stripes

Applications

Municipal Gas Networks

Urban and suburban natural gas distribution

Industrial Gas Supply

Factory and industrial facility connections

Residential Connections

Service lines to homes and buildings
